GeeksforGeeks is a computer science portal. It is the best platform to lean programming.

A Braqqet token is the smallest unit of a property that can be bought or invested in. Braqqet offers a minimum $10 token amount on every property.

Capital gain on property value: since a property value generally increases over time, the value your Braqqet token also appreciates with respect to the increase in property value too.

Rental income: Net rental income( total rental income minus all operational costs) is paid to all investors. This payment is distributed based on the percentage of Braqqet tokens in a given property.!

Rental income on investment is distributed to every Braqqet token holder at the end of every month.

Compared to other investment options real estate has a relatively low risk attached, there are some risks to take into consideration when making an investment decision some of which are:

Long term vacancy: Having a property without any tenant for an extended period in time would significantly reduce the rental income on that property. For this sole reason Braqqet invests in properties in cities and neighborhoods where we believe rental demand is high and sustainable as a way to minimize this risk.

Lease default: Situations occur when a tenant or occupant defaults on lease or is unable to pay their rent, this will eventually lead to terminating our agreement with them. To minimize this risk a thorough selection process is carried out on every prospective occupant.

Operational expense risk: When the operational costs of a property increases faster than the rental income growth this could lead to lower net rental income which in essence reduces the returns on your Braqqet investment.
